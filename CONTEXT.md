# LifeSim Context

Shared project language for LifeSim's game design and production planning. This glossary keeps domain terms precise so tickets, roadmap decisions, and implementation reviews use the same words.

## Language

**Vertical Slice A**:
The first playable LifeSim slice: a 20-30 minute nonlinear city prologue that proves the core loop of needs, work, money, food, NPC interaction, gifting, sleep, save/load, and the game's diary/memory-driven narrative style.
_Avoid_: tech demo, Phase 1 demo, city prototype, full Act 1

**Slice District**:
The single city district used by Vertical Slice A. It should feel like a small lived-in neighborhood with enough room and points of interest to avoid feeling like a cramped test scene.
_Avoid_: test room, micro map, multi-district slice

**City Prologue**:
The cold open of LifeSim, set on the protagonist's first day in the city before the story flashes back to the childhood chapter. It presents the city as the question that the hometown memories will later contextualize.
_Avoid_: chronological opening, tutorial-only intro

**Childhood Chapter**:
The flashback chapter after the City Prologue, focused on the protagonist's last summer at home and the relationships that give emotional meaning to leaving.
_Avoid_: optional backstory, lore dump
