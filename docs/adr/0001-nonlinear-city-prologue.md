# Nonlinear City Prologue

LifeSim will open with a 20-30 minute city prologue instead of a chronological childhood opening. This cold open introduces the playable core loop, visual tone, diary/memory narrative style, and the protagonist's present loneliness first, then transitions through a quiet stargazing moment into the beginning of the childhood chapter to show what shaped the protagonist before leaving.

**Considered Options**

- Start chronologically in the hometown childhood chapter.
- Start directly in the city as the full Act 1.
- Start with a short nonlinear city prologue, then flash back to childhood.

**Consequences**

Vertical Slice A is now the City Prologue, not a generic systems demo. Early tickets should prioritize one large-enough city district, a complete one-day loop, and a stargazing transition that zooms from the city night sky into the same sky above the childhood setting; multi-district travel, farming, phone systems, and romance remain out of scope until the prologue loop feels strong.
